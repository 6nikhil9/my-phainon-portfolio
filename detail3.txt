### ðŸ§  Design Recommendations for the UI

Based on the **Poster Image** (`here-comes-the-design...jpg`) and the **Character Art**:

1. **Layout:** Use **Asymmetry**. Don't just do a standard grid. Have elements overlap (like how the text "PHAINON" overlaps the character in the poster).
2. **Typography:** Mix **Big Bold Sans-Serif** (like the "PHAINON" text in the poster) with **Elegant Serifs** (for body text/quotes).
* *Header Font:* "Oswald" or "Anton" (Tall, impact)
* *Subtext Font:* "Cinzel" (Fantasy/Regal)


3. **Gold Accents:** Don't overuse gold. Use it for **active states** (hovering over a skill) or **key lines** (separating sections). Let the darkness breathe.
4. **Particles:** Implementing a subtle "Gold Dust" particle effect in the background (using `tsparticles` or simple CSS animations) would perfectly match the "dissolving" effect seen in the character art.
